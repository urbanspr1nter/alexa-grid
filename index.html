Alexa Grid System

A simple grid system for those who do not want the grid-features of a heavy-weight CSS framework.

Example Usage

<div class="grid">
 <div class="col-2">Nav<div>
 <div class="col-8">Content<div>
 <div class="col-2">Side<div>
</div>
 
This will produce:
 
Nav
Content
Side
